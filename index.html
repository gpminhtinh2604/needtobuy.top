Grocery List – A Simple Grocery Shopping List App

Posted on October 15, 2017 by minhtinh2604

 

Grocery List app – Will not forget when shopping with Grocery list.
We often remember what to buy and go to the market or grocery store. Then we forget to buy something when returns home.
Or we note into the paper and then forget to take it when go shopping.

This is a simple app to make list grocery that we need to buy.
– Very little screen, very simple to make a grocery list.
– Take it and go to the market, grocery store or online grocery store to check when buying.
⇒ We will not forget to buy something, or buy too much than we need.
